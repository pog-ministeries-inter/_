Vision

Isaiah 61 vs 1 to 11

  • heal the broken hearted
  • preach the good news to the poor and aflicted
    • free those who are bound or in captive(both spiritually and physically)
  • To display his splendor so that he may be glorified
  • To raise more ministers of the Lord

Purpose

  • To get people to experience the glory of God in their lives and realise the rewards of putting their trust in God. For the world to see what God can do which is beyond man's ability, so that they experience the power of God hence the name "Power of God"
